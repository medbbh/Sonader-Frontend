Presentation

The National Company for Rural Development is a public entity with a commercial and industrial nature. It has legal personality and financial independence, established in 1975 to promote irrigated agriculture in Mauritania. It is involved in formulating, implementing, monitoring, and evaluating integrated development policies along the Senegal River and its tributaries and branches, serving all national and foreign, public or private actors. In pursuit of its assigned tasks, the National Company for Rural Development "SONADER" undertakes the mission of promoting irrigated agriculture in the riverbank region to increase agricultural yields and improve their quality.

48 years of extensive experience

"SONADER" Company has more than 48 years of extensive experience in training and supporting farmers. SONADER possesses a rich archive of studies and field surveys.

The company has reclaimed and utilized over 30.000 hectares of "collective farms" and is currently working on reclaiming an additional 1.120 hectares."

100 Km of waterways and irrigation water to 12,250 h

The company is responsible for the maintenance and supervision of over 100 kilometers of waterways. The company provides irrigation water to 12,250 hectares.

In the field of guidance, the company is currently mentoring more than 956 agricultural cooperatives in the riverbank region. The company has provided training to 2.322 agricultural producers in the past years.
